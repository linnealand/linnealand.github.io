Lifestyle App
Bamboo

A cross-platform mobile app for tracking diet and exercise.

Background

Bamboo is an intuitive mobile app for tracking diet and exercise and helping you find the best ways to achieve your goals. The app is designed to provide relevant and accurate information, while minimizing manual input from the user.
This was a group project for CS 307: Software Engineering. We followed the Scrum methodology and completed three sprints over the course of the semester.

Features

  • Health profile including allergy information
  • Automatic nutrition calculation for meals via Spoonacular API
    • Enter meal by recipe name, recipe link, or ingredients
  • Automatic calories burned calculation for exercises using MET values
  • Set diet and exercise related goals and track progress
  • Graphs tracking diet and exercise information
  • Personal meals recommendation
  • Personal exercise recommendation
  • Optional notifications
